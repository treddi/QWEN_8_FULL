World Time Converter welcomes links from other websites. This policy outlines the guidelines for linking to our website and using our content.

You are welcome to link to any page on World Time Converter from your website, blog, or application, provided that:

  • The link does not imply endorsement of your products or services
  • Your website does not contain unlawful, harmful, or offensive content

Linking Guidelines

Deep Linking: You may link directly to any specific page on our website, including our time converter tool, city pages, or informational content.

Framing: You may not frame our website or any of its content within your website without our prior written permission.

Use of Logos and Trademarks: You may not use the World Time Converter logo or trademarks without our express written permission, except to link to our website using standard text links.

Attribution Requirements

When linking to World Time Converter, we request but do not require the following attribution:

  • Use descriptive anchor text such as "World Time Converter" or "Time Zone Converter"
  • Link to our homepage (https://worldtimeconverter.com) or specific relevant pages
  • Do not use automated scripts to create links without permission

Prohibited Linking

You must not link to World Time Converter if:

  • Your website contains adult content, gambling, or illegal activities
  • Your website promotes hate speech, discrimination, or violence
  • Your website infringes on intellectual property rights

  • You engage in link spamming or automated link building
