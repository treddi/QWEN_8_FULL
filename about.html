About Us

Welcome to World Time Converter, your trusted companion for accurate time conversion across the globe. Since 1995, we've been helping millions of users convert time between cities, schedule international meetings, and stay connected across time zones.

Our Mission

We believe that connecting people across different time zones should be simple and accurate. Our mission is to provide the most reliable, user-friendly time conversion tools available online, completely free of charge.

What We Offer

  • Accurate time conversion for 500+ cities worldwide
  • Automatic daylight saving time adjustments
  • Real-time updates and precise calculations
  • Mobile-friendly design for on-the-go conversions
  • No registration required - completely free to use

Our Technology

We use the latest timezone databases and atomic clock synchronization to ensure every conversion is accurate to the second. Our system automatically accounts for daylight saving time changes, leap years, and historical timezone adjustments.

Global Reach

With users in over 150 countries, World Time Converter has become an essential tool for businesses, travelers, remote workers, and anyone who needs to coordinate across time zones. We support multiple languages and continue to expand our city database based on user feedback.

Commitment to Privacy

We respect your privacy. Our time converter works entirely in your browser - no personal data is stored or transmitted to our servers. You can convert times confidently and privately.
